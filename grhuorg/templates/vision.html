Vision, Mission Statement and Values

Vision Statement:

"Refugees living with hope again."

Mission Statment:

"GRHU is committed to creating awareness of the hopeless position of all refugees victims of war and violence in Africa. GRHU works to raise the quality of life through income generating projects, skills training, and by providing basic necessities to give hope for a better future."

Statement of Values:

"GRHU will help every refugee regardless of ethnicity, gender, or religion. The words of 1 John 3:17 call us to help everyone in need: 'But if anyone has the world's goods and sees his brother in need, yet closes his heart against him, how does God's love abide in him?' This call to help extends not only to our neighbour, but to each and every person on the planet."
