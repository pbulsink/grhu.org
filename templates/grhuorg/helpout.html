Help Out with GRHU

So you want to help out with GRHU? Maybe you want to help at an event, or you have an idea on another event that GRHU could do, or you have ideas on running your own! We're really excited to hear about all the ways you want to help out, and we'll be sure to find you a place with a job to do. If you need volunteer hours for school or any other reason, we'll be happy to sign off on that as well. No matter what your motivation, please send us an email at info@grhu.org and we'll be sure to gt back to you with how you can help us!

If you'd like to help out by attending an event, find out when the next one is here.

For information on donating, please visit the donation page.
